On the ROOMinate designer page, click the icon with two people on it. A popup should appear with 'Host' and 'Join' tabs. If you would like to host, copy the id on the 'Host' tab and send it to your friend. Your friend will then click on the icon with two people and toggle over to the 'Join' tab. Once your friend is on the 'Join' tab, have your friend paste the id you've sent into the 'Enter a Host ID' textbox and click the purple 'connect' button. You will then be set to do google docs like collaboration. If you are still having trouble, reach out to us via the contact form page and we will get back to you within 1-2 buisness days!
